About Us

EeeSports Limited is a fully Nigerian owned business with world-class operational facilities for the satisfaction of our clients. Eeesports Limited has a strict policy of dealing only with sports products that have been certified worldwide for safety and quality. We also ensure our customers are always offered defect-free goods at all times.

We are a one-stop brand for sports wears and equipment, Bikes, and Treadmills. We consult, build, supply, and maintain everything you require for effective health and fitness. At EeeSports, we help you achieve fitness by providing you with the most suitable equipment at competitive prices.

Our Vision

At Eeesports Limited, we are committed to being the Global Leader in the health and fitness sector by providing innovative work out machines. We are committed to providing excellent services that aim to promote and improve the health of mankind.

Our Mission

To be the number one solution and global leader in the production of innovative health and fitness equipment.
